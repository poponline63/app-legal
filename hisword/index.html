Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

His Word ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

His Word is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best experience:

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers.
  • Local Storage Only: All your preferences, favorite verses, and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • No Account Required: You don't need to create an account or provide any personal details to use our app.

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Your selected spiritual categories
  • Favorite Bible verses
  • Notification preferences and timing
  • App settings and preferences
  • Premium subscription status (if applicable)

Notifications

If you choose to enable notifications, we will:

  • Send daily verse reminders at your chosen time
  • Only use your device's local notification system
  • Never send your notification preferences or timing to external servers

No Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any of your information with third parties because:

  • We don't collect personal data
  • All data remains on your device
  • We have no user accounts or databases

Premium Features & Subscriptions

If you purchase a premium subscription:

  • Payment processing is handled by Apple's App Store or Google Play Store. We never see or store your payment card details.
  • We use RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management service, to process purchases, verify your subscription status, and restore purchases across your devices.
  • To do this, RevenueCat receives a randomly generated app user identifier and your purchase/transaction details from the app store. It does not receive your name, email, or payment card information.
  • You can review RevenueCat's privacy practices at revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • Subscription management and cancellation are handled through your app store account.

Data Security

Your data security is important to us:

  • All data is stored locally using your device's secure storage
  • The only information that leaves your device is the subscription/purchase data needed to process and verify a premium subscription (handled by Apple and RevenueCat, as described above)
  • Your favorite verses, categories, and preferences never leave your device

Children's Privacy

His Word is suitable for all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for children's data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on our website. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • You can reset your preferences in the app settings
  • You can disable notifications at any time
