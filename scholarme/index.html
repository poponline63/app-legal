Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Introduction

ScholarMe ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our scholarship finder application.

Information We Collect

Profile Information (Stored Locally Only)

When you create your profile, we collect the following information, which is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Name and educational background
  • Academic information (GPA, major, grade level)
  • Demographic information (optional, used only for scholarship matching)
  • State of residence
  • Scholarship preferences and saved scholarships

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our app, including:

  • App performance metrics
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Error reports (without personal information)

How We Use Your Information

  • Scholarship Matching: Your profile information is used locally on your device to match you with relevant scholarships
  • Personalization: We customize your experience based on your saved preferences
  • App Improvement: Anonymous usage data helps us enhance the app's functionality

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All personal information is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. We do not transmit your personal profile data to our servers.

Scholarship Database: Scholarship information is publicly available data sourced from educational institutions and foundations. This information is provided for informational purposes only.

Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data that may be transmitted, including encryption and secure communication protocols.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With your explicit consent
  • Anonymous, aggregated data for research or improvement purposes

Subscriptions & Payments

If you purchase a premium subscription:

  • Payment is processed by Apple's App Store or Google Play Store. We never see or store your payment card details.
  • We use RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management service, to process purchases, verify your subscription status, and restore purchases across your devices.
  • RevenueCat receives a randomly generated app user identifier and your purchase/transaction details from the app store. It does not receive your name, profile, or payment card information. See revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • You manage or cancel your subscription through your app store account.

Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to external scholarship websites and applications. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before submitting any personal information.

Push Notifications

With your permission, we may send push notifications about:

  • Upcoming scholarship deadlines
  • New scholarships matching your profile
  • Important app updates

You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for students 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

Data Retention

Your profile data remains on your device until you choose to delete it or uninstall the app. You can clear your data at any time through the app settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored profile information
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your profile and all associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or by posting the updated policy with a new effective date.
